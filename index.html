A Story of Grief & Grace

Trauern is an independent German-language feature exploring the quiet geography of loss and how mourning reshapes identity, memory, and the will to persist. Directed by Andrew Müller, the film is a meditation on what endures when everything else dissolves. Shot in austere, intimate frames, Trauern asks: what does it mean to survive your own sorrow?
